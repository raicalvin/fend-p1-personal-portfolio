Modal Analysis Android App

×

Modal Analysis Application

Android Application

This Android application was developed for a former professor at my undergraduate institution. One of my structural engineering professor wanted an application where he could use to teach concepts in earthquake engineering, modal analysis, and building movement behavior.

This was my very first Android app and I had to use two libraries (GraphView and JAMA) to (1) display a graph that would depict building movement in different modes and (2) use a JAMA library to perform the matrix algebra required to obtain Eigensolutions representing the buildings' mode, frequencies, periods, mass participation factor, response time, and more.

The application itself allows students to enter in mass and stiffness values for column supports for a typical 4-story building the the program will calculate a variety of eqarthquake engineering parameters based on these values. The student can also flip through the modes of the building seeing how the floors would behave in different mdoes.
